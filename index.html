In the 16th century, Zanzibar became a real gourmet paradise. Portuguese traders from South America and India brought hundreds of varieties of aromatic plants and spices to the island. Since then, it has sprawled hectares of botanical gardens, where various spices and fruits grow.

On these outlandish plantations, we offer you a delicious and spectacular excursion. You will see with your own eyes how they grow, learn all the secrets of their use, and taste seasonal spices and fruits right from the garden.

You will taste cloves, lemongrass, nutmeg, cinnamon, turmeric, vanilla, papaya, jack fruit, cardamom, cassava, oranges. And then a real Swahili lunch will be prepared specifically for you. You can enjoy homemade food made according to traditional Zanzibar recipes.

But that's not all. Full immersion in the local flavors awaits you. You will witness how a young boy deftly and without protection climbs a palm tree 20 meters high to cut a coconut and treat you to the freshest coconut milk. Such a daredevil is called a butterfly fight. And this is an incredible sight that will take your breath away.

Don't miss the opportunity to take an atmospheric guided tour of the world's most impressive spice garden.
